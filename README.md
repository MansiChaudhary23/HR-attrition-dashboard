#  HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built using the **IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Dataset** to analyze workforce attrition patterns and identify key factors influencing employee turnover. The dashboard provides HR professionals and business leaders with actionable insights through executive KPIs, interactive visualizations, and data-driven recommendations.

---

## 📸 Dashboard Preview

![HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard](dashboard.png)


---

#  Project Objectives

- Monitor overall employee attrition and workforce statistics.
- Identify departments and job roles with the highest employee turnover.
- Analyze the impact of overtime on employee attrition.
- Evaluate employee departures across different job satisfaction levels.
- Support HR decision-making through interactive data visualization.

---

#  Dashboard Features

###  Executive KPI Cards
-  Total Employees
-  Attrition Rate
-  Employees Left
-  Average Tenure
-  Average Age

###  Interactive Visualizations
- Employee Attrition by Department
- Employee Turnover by Job Role
- Employee Departures by Job Satisfaction
- Overtime vs Employee Attrition Analysis

###  Interactive Filters
- Job Role
- Department
- Education Field
- Gender

###  Business Insights
- Research & Development records the highest employee attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians experience the highest employee turnover.
- Employees working overtime show considerably higher attrition rates.
- Employee departures vary across different job satisfaction levels.

#  Dataset

**IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Dataset**

The dataset contains employee demographic, job, salary, performance, and satisfaction attributes used to analyze workforce attrition trends.

---

#  Key Insights

- **Attrition Rate:** 16.1%
- **Research & Development** department experiences the highest employee attrition.
- **Laboratory Technicians** have the highest turnover among all job roles.
- Employees working **overtime** are significantly more likely to leave the organization.
- Workforce attrition varies across different job satisfaction levels.

---

#  Business Recommendation

- Review overtime policies to reduce employee burnout.
- Strengthen retention initiatives for high-risk job roles.
- Improve employee engagement and workplace satisfaction.
- Focus retention efforts on departments with the highest attrition.
